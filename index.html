George Autoshop

Welcome to George Autoshop! Either you’re a new or regular costumer either way we are so happy that you made it to our new website!

George Autoshop is a family owned company started 1950’s by Bobby Brown. Bobby got his passion for cars from his father George. Georges joy and glory in life was his blue Mercedes-benz Cabriolet. George use to bring Bobby to car meets and dragracing all over Australia since before he could walk.

That is Bobby’s greatest childhood memories! it was like a candy store with the coolest and most beautiful cars in all kind off colors and shapes.

When they weren’t on the rood to the meet’s George taught Bobby to build his own cars and when Bobby was fifteen, he finished build his first own car it was a modified Chrysler Airflow.

Bobby’s father passed away to early and left all his assets to Bobby and before he past away he told Bobby to fallow his dream.

So… Bobby started George Autoshop to honor is fathers wish and named the shop after him as well!

From the beginning were a repair shop for classic, vintage, and antique cars. But we notice that people often asked if we had any cars for sale, so we began looking for older cars to renovate and rebuild, so in 1959 we started to sell classic, vintage, and antique cars in the shop.

Of cause we work with repairs of all kind off cars BUT we have a special kind off love for the older ones.

We are proud to say that George Autoshop still are owned and operated by three generations of Browns and in-laws. We are proud to offer the best combination of affordability, special knowledge of older cars and the best customer service possible.

Do you have any questions don’t hesitate to ask, we love talking about our passion!
